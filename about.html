Who We Are.

Our Mission

Providing compassionate care and rehabilitation for mental health in Ethiopia.

At Gergesenon Mental Rehabilitation Center, we believe in the dignity and potential of every individual. Our comprehensive approach combines professional mental health care with cultural sensitivity and community integration.

Through dedicated support and innovative rehabilitation programs, we help individuals recover and reintegrate into their communities with dignity and purpose.

Our Journey

A legacy of healing and hope since 2005

2005

Foundation

Gergesenon Mental Rehabilitation Center was founded in Addis Ababa, focusing on support and rehabilitation for individuals with severe mental illnesses.

2010

Community Outreach

Expanded services to include family education programs, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with local clinics.

2020

Rehabilitation Programs

Introduced structured rehabilitation plans, integrating traditional and modern mental health therapies for long-term recovery.

Meles Gergesenon

Founder & Director

Driven by compassion and a vision for accessible mental health care, Meles established Gergesenon Mental Rehabilitation Center to provide shelter, treatment, and dignity for those facing severe mental challenges. His leadership emphasizes culturally-sensitive care and community integration.
